# SEO Keyword Research Process
> End-to-End Keyword Research Framework for `{ website }`

---

## Input → Target Website

Begin with the client's website URL. This anchors every research decision that follows.

---

## Step 01 — AI-Powered Business Profile

**Tool:** Custom AI Prompt → Website Content

Run the following prompt on the website content:

> *"You are a clear, intellectual Data-based Marketeer with a keen eye for business analysis and strategy. Your expertise lies in distilling complex information into actionable insights. Your communication style is concise yet comprehensive, always grounded in data and objective analysis. Your task is to analyze the provided website content and generate a detailed business profile. This profile should capture the essence of the business, its target market, its operational model, and the Tonality of the brand. Your analysis should be thorough, drawing insights directly from the given text to ensure accuracy and relevance. { website }"*

**Output:**
- Brand identity & tone of voice
- Target market
- Operational model
- Services offered
- Geographic focus

---

## Step 02 — Deep-Read Business Profile

Manually review the generated profile. Internalize:
- What they sell
- Who they serve
- How they position themselves
- What makes them different

---

## Step 03 — Identify Core Keywords / Seed Keywords / Niche Entities

Extract the primary topics, money keywords, and niche entities that define the business.

These become the **foundation for all subsequent research**.

---

## Step 04 — Google SERP Analysis → Find Competitors

Search seed keywords + money page topics on Google.

Identify who's ranking and split into two buckets:

### 🎯 Direct Competitors
- Same services / products
- Same target market
- Same geography / country
- Check: website content & offers
- Check: service areas

### 📄 Organic Competitors
- Authority content / blogs
- Related niche content
- Not necessarily same service
- Check: informational content
- Check: related topics & clusters

---

## Step 05 — Analyze Direct Competitors

For each direct competitor:
- Review website content
- Review services and offers
- Note business serve area or country

---

## Step 06 — Analyze Organic Competitors

For each organic competitor:
- Review authority content and blogs
- Identify content related to your business and niche
- Note topics and clusters they dominate

## Phase 1 — Reverse-Engineer High-Converting URLs

**Tool:** Ahrefs → Site Explorer

Before looking for new keywords, identify which existing pages are already driving the most valuable conversions — so you know which topics deserve the largest share of your content budget.

### Process:
1. Identify if the website has existing content, money pages, or service pages
2. Find the right keywords for existing URLs — these will be **category pages** or **service pages** depending on the website
3. Establish website baseline — these top keywords represent your most lucrative **Core Topics**
4. Mathematically determine which verticals to dedicate the majority of your topical map toward

---

## Phase 2 — Build the Topical Map

**Tool:** Ahrefs → Keywords Explorer + Site Explorer + Content Gap

Now that you know your core topics, build out **Pillar** and **Cluster** pages to dominate each vertical.

Find keywords in the form of **TOFU / MOFU / BOFU** using three methods:

---

### Method 01 — Competitor Top Pages (Site Explorer)

1. Enter each competitor website in Ahrefs Site Explorer
2. Check their **Top Pages**
3. Check their **Top Keyword Rankings**
4. Filter: is it related to our `{ website / business }`?
5. Tag each keyword as **TOFU / MOFU / BOFU**
6. Repeat this process for all direct and organic competitors until you have a large pool of topics and keywords

---

### Method 02 — Seed Keywords (Keywords Explorer)

1. Open Ahrefs **Keywords Explorer**
2. Input seed keywords / core topics (e.g. `"seed keyword"`, `"core topic"`)
3. Use the **Matching Terms** report → get thousands of keyword variations
4. Group by **Parent Topic** using the toggle in the left sidebar
   - **Parent Topic** → becomes your **Pillar Page**
   - **Grouped keywords underneath** → become your **Cluster Pages**
5. Click the **Questions** tab → filter for who / what / where / why / how queries
6. Export these to form **Informational Cluster Pages** that internally link back to the main pillar

---

### Method 03 — Content Gap Analysis

1. Go to Ahrefs **Site Explorer → Content Gap Tool**
2. Input **3–5 direct competitors** who operate in the same vertical as your highest-converting topic
3. Include your own website
4. Set filter: keywords where **at least 2–3 competitors are ranking but you are not**
5. Export the list
6. Sort by **Intent, not Volume** — categorize into:
   - Informational
   - Tutorials
   - Comparisons
   - Features
7. Tag each as **BOFU / MOFU / TOFU** or related to your niche

---

## Consolidate — Merge All Sources

Merge all findings from the three methods:

> **Manual Research → Competitor Research → Content Gap (our site vs competitors)**

- De-duplicate keywords
- Validate relevance to business
- Remove irrelevant topics
- Check for missing keywords not yet covered
