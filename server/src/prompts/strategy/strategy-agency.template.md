# Agency Strategy Template

## Strategic Priorities (Agency-Specific)

1. **Service Page Architecture** — Build detailed, keyword-optimized service pages for each offering (e.g., "[service type] agency", "[service] services", "[industry] [service]")
2. **Case Study & Results Content** — Publish detailed case studies with metrics, before/after data, and client testimonials to demonstrate E-E-A-T
3. **Industry Vertical Pages** — Create dedicated pages for each industry served ("[service] for [industry]") to capture niche commercial searches
4. **Thought Leadership & Original Research** — Publish original data, surveys, and benchmark reports to earn backlinks and establish authority
5. **Geographic Targeting** — If the agency serves specific regions, build "[service] in [city/region]" pages with localized proof points

## Content Mix (Recommended)

| Funnel Stage | Content Type | Share of Effort |
|---|---|---|
| TOFU | Blog posts, industry reports, benchmarks, webinars | 30% |
| MOFU | Case studies, service deep-dives, process explainers | 35% |
| BOFU | Service pages, pricing, consultation CTAs, proposals | 25% |
| Retention | Client resources, reporting dashboards, newsletters | 10% |

## Keyword Prioritization (Agency Signals)

- **High priority:** "[service] agency", "[service] company", "[service] services" keywords with commercial intent
- **Quick wins:** Existing service pages ranking 4-20 that need content depth, testimonials, or schema
- **Industry verticals:** "[industry] [service]" combinations the agency serves but doesn't rank for
- **Competitor differentiation:** "[competitor] alternative", "[competitor] vs [our brand]" comparison content
- **Long-tail expertise:** Specific methodology or framework keywords that demonstrate deep expertise

## KPI Benchmarks (Agency)

| Metric | 90-Day Target | 6-Month Target |
|---|---|---|
| Organic leads (form submissions) | +20% | +50% |
| Service page rankings (top 10) | +30% | +70% |
| Case study page traffic | +40% | +100% |
| Domain Rating improvement | +4 DR | +10 DR |
| Branded search volume | +15% | +35% |

## Risk Factors

- Agency keywords are highly competitive; differentiation through case studies and proof points is essential
- Generic service pages without specifics (results, process, team) underperform; each needs 1500+ words
- Over-reliance on blog content without commercial page optimization leaves revenue on the table
- Client confidentiality may limit case study depth; use anonymized data with permission where needed
