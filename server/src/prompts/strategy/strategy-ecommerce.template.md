# E-Commerce Strategy Template

## Strategic Priorities (E-Commerce-Specific)

1. **Product & Category Page SEO** — Optimize category pages as the primary ranking targets; ensure unique descriptions, proper H-tag hierarchy, and faceted navigation handling
2. **Product Schema & Rich Results** — Implement Product, Review, FAQ, and BreadcrumbList schema to maximize SERP real estate (stars, price, availability)
3. **Buying Guide & Comparison Content** — Create comprehensive buying guides targeting commercial-intent keywords ("[product] buying guide", "best [product] for [use case]")
4. **Programmatic SEO at Scale** — Leverage product data for template-based pages: brand pages, attribute pages, "best [product] under $X" pages
5. **Technical SEO for Large Catalogs** — Address crawl budget, pagination, canonical tags, and index bloat from faceted navigation

## Content Mix (Recommended)

| Funnel Stage | Content Type | Share of Effort |
|---|---|---|
| TOFU | Buying guides, how-to articles, trend roundups | 25% |
| MOFU | Category pages, comparison pages, reviews | 35% |
| BOFU | Product pages, deal/sale pages, bundles | 30% |
| Retention | Post-purchase guides, warranty info, care guides | 10% |

## Keyword Prioritization (E-Commerce Signals)

- **High priority:** Category-level keywords with high volume + transactional intent (e.g., "buy [product]", "[product] online")
- **Quick wins:** Product pages ranking 4-20 that need meta/content optimization
- **Long-tail capture:** Specific product attributes ("[color] [product]", "[brand] [model]")
- **Seasonal:** Plan content for seasonal peaks (holiday, back-to-school, Black Friday)

## KPI Benchmarks (E-Commerce)

| Metric | 90-Day Target | 6-Month Target |
|---|---|---|
| Organic revenue | +15% | +40% |
| Organic sessions | +20% | +50% |
| Category page rankings (top 10) | +25% | +60% |
| Product pages indexed | 95% crawled | 98% crawled |
| Rich result CTR improvement | +10% | +25% |

## Risk Factors

- Large catalogs face crawl budget constraints; prioritize high-value pages in sitemap
- Out-of-stock pages must return 200 (not 404) with "notify me" CTAs to retain equity
- Faceted navigation can create thousands of thin/duplicate pages if not properly canonicalized
- Seasonal keyword strategies require 60-90 day lead time before peak periods
