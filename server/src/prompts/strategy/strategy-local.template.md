# Local Business Strategy Template

## Strategic Priorities (Local-Specific)

1. **Google Business Profile Optimization** — Ensure NAP consistency, categories, attributes, and regular posts are optimized to dominate the local 3-pack
2. **Local Landing Pages** — Build service + location pages targeting "[service] in [city/neighborhood]" and "[service] near me" patterns
3. **Review & Reputation Strategy** — Develop a systematic review generation program; reviews are a top local ranking factor
4. **Local Schema Markup** — Implement LocalBusiness, Service, and FAQ schema on all relevant pages
5. **Local Link Building** — Pursue citations, local directories, chamber of commerce, sponsorships, and community partnerships

## Content Mix (Recommended)

| Funnel Stage | Content Type | Share of Effort |
|---|---|---|
| TOFU | Local guides, community content, "best of" lists | 25% |
| MOFU | Service pages, FAQs, before/after galleries | 35% |
| BOFU | Contact, booking, quote request, pricing pages | 30% |
| Retention | Review solicitation, email nurture content | 10% |

## Keyword Prioritization (Local Signals)

- **High priority:** "[service] + [city]" and "[service] near me" keywords with local pack presence
- **Quick wins:** Keywords where the domain ranks 4-20 that have local intent
- **Service expansion:** Adjacent services the business offers but doesn't rank for
- **Neighborhood targeting:** Sub-city geographic modifiers for larger metro areas

## KPI Benchmarks (Local Business)

| Metric | 90-Day Target | 6-Month Target |
|---|---|---|
| Google Business Profile views | +40% | +100% |
| Local 3-pack appearances | +5 keywords | +15 keywords |
| Phone calls from organic | +20% | +50% |
| Direction requests | +25% | +60% |
| Reviews count | +15 new reviews | +40 new reviews |

## Risk Factors

- Local results are volatile; competitor actions and Google algorithm changes can shift rankings quickly
- NAP inconsistencies across directories can suppress local visibility
- Duplicate content risk with multi-location pages — each must have 40%+ unique content
