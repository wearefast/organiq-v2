# Publisher / Media Strategy Template

## Strategic Priorities (Publisher-Specific)

1. **Topical Authority Clusters** — Build deep content clusters around 5-7 core verticals to establish E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
2. **Google Discover & News Optimization** — Optimize for Discover with high-quality images, compelling titles, and timely content; implement NewsArticle schema
3. **Evergreen + Trending Mix** — Balance evergreen pillar content (stable traffic) with timely/trending pieces (traffic spikes + Discover eligibility)
4. **Author Authority Pages** — Build dedicated author pages with credentials, social links, and article archives to strengthen E-E-A-T signals
5. **Monetization-Aware SEO** — Prioritize keywords by RPM potential (ad revenue, affiliate commission, sponsored content opportunity)

## Content Mix (Recommended)

| Funnel Stage | Content Type | Share of Effort |
|---|---|---|
| TOFU | News, trending topics, opinion pieces | 30% |
| MOFU | In-depth guides, explainers, analysis | 35% |
| BOFU | Product reviews, "best of" roundups, deal pages | 25% |
| Retention | Newsletters, push notification content | 10% |

## Keyword Prioritization (Publisher Signals)

- **High priority:** High-volume informational keywords where the site can establish topical authority
- **Quick wins:** Existing articles ranking 4-20 that need content refreshes or improved internal linking
- **Trending capture:** Set up workflows to publish on trending topics within 24-48 hours
- **Affiliate/commercial:** "Best [product]" and review keywords with high CPC (affiliate revenue proxy)
- **AI citation targets:** Keywords where AI assistants cite web sources — optimize for citability

## KPI Benchmarks (Publisher)

| Metric | 90-Day Target | 6-Month Target |
|---|---|---|
| Organic pageviews | +30% | +75% |
| Google Discover impressions | +50% | +150% |
| Top 10 keyword positions | +40% | +100% |
| Average session duration | +15% | +25% |
| Revenue per 1000 sessions (RPM) | +10% | +20% |

## Risk Factors

- Google's Helpful Content Update penalizes thin, AI-generated, or low-value content at scale
- Over-reliance on trending content creates traffic volatility; maintain a 60% evergreen baseline
- Author E-E-A-T is increasingly important; anonymous or generic bylines suppress rankings
- Core Web Vitals are critical for publishers — ad-heavy pages must still pass CWV thresholds
